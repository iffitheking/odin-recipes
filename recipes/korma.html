Sindhi Biryani

Ingrediants

  • Meat on Bones: 1 KG
  • Rice: 300g
  • Plain Yogurt: 300g
  • 2-3 tablespoons
  • Oil-Ghee 175ml
  • Shan Korma Masala Mix

STEPS OF COOKING

  1. Heat oil, fry onions until light golden, remove and crush.
  2. In the same oil, add meat, yogurt and Shan Korma Mix. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Add julienne ginger and Â½-1 cup water for gravy. Cover and cook on low heat until meat is tender.
  4. Stir-in crushed onions. Cover and cook on low heat for 5-10 minutes.
